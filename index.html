Local News LLC

Local News LLC is a reliable source that delivers authentic news directly from communities in SE Franklin & NE Washington Chicago Counties. Our coverage encompasses a wide range of topics, including businesses, non-profits, schools, school sports, churches, clubs, and municipalities. We are dedicated to providing comprehensive local news.

What sets us apart from other media companies is our unique background. Local News LLC was established by a group of local residents who recognized the necessity for trustworthy and verified news within their community. These individuals came together and committed their time and resources to create a platform for disseminating information about community events. Our team consists of a small yet highly experienced group of individuals who are deeply invested in the well-being of our community. Our primary objective is not only to deliver essential news but also to highlight the positive developments occurring throughout the region.
